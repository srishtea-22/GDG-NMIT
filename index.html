About Events Team
Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology - Bengaluru, India

Who are we?

Google Developer Student Clubs, Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology is a college based community functioning with an aim to promote peer-to-peer learning with an active usage of tools and technologies offered by Google. Here in the community, we learn and further our developers' skills with an intent to abide by the idea of putting knowledge to practical use.

We, in the community, conduct webinars, hands-on workshops, tutorials, informative talks and more to engage with the audience and grow the community at large.

What we do

We build

The Tech Team drives innovation in our club, building and maintaining digital tools and projects. We empower members through hands-on workshops and projects, fostering skills in coding and technology.

We design

The Design Team shapes the visual identity of our club, creating graphics, branding, and event materials. We bring ideas to life through creativity and design, enhancing engagement and communication.

We connect

The Content and PR Team crafts our club's voice and manages outreach. From creating engaging posts to building connections, we focus on spreading awareness and keeping our community informed and inspired.
